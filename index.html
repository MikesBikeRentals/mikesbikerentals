Discover the Finger Lakes by Bike

Located in the heart of Watkins Glen, Mike’s Bike Rentals offers a gateway to the natural beauty of the Finger Lakes. Our bikes are perfect for exploring scenic destinations like the Wine Trail, Seneca Lake, and Watkins Glen State Park.

Catherine Valley Trail

Running nearly 12 miles through lush forests, past streams, and beside vineyards, the Catherine Valley Trail is one of the most breathtaking bike routes in New York. Start your adventure just blocks from our shop!
