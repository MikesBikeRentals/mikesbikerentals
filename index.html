About Us

Originally founded by Mike Harvey, Mike’s Bike Rentals is now operated by Richard Swinnerton and proudly continues to serve locals and tourists alike from our location near the southern tip of Seneca Lake. Whether you're cruising the village or exploring the Finger Lakes trails, we’ve got the perfect ride for you.
